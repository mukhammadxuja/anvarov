Our customer

When using the system, you will always know how many sales your
company made in a certain period of time. In addition, you will fire
less productive employees and replace them with productive ones, and
you will understand what your employees are doing during their working
hours. Now it is possible to automate the work of sales
representatives 100% and control their work.

Tech stack: Reactjs,
Firebase, Tailwind

Reactjs and tailwind css were used for the frontend, and Firebase was
used for the backend. lazydev ui kits are used as UI components. You
can see full information about Lazydev in the projects page.
